# Meeting Summary Skill

You now have expertise in structuring raw meeting transcripts into organized, actionable meeting records. This skill covers participant extraction, topic segmentation, key-point distillation, action-item tracking, and follow-up management.

## What is a Meeting Summary?

A meeting summary transforms unstructured dialogue into:
- **Basic Info**: Topic, time, location, recorder
- **Participants**: Who attended and their roles
- **Agenda Topics**: What was discussed, in order
- **Key Points**: Critical statements, decisions, and disagreements per topic
- **Action Items**: Assigned tasks with owners, deadlines, and priorities
- **Follow-ups**: Unresolved issues and next meeting preview
